💡 Pro Tips & Tricks

  • Smart File Naming:Downloaded files are beautifully named QR-SnG_[your-content].png, making your downloads folder organized and easy to search.
  • Large Text Hosting:Trying to fit a long paragraph into a QR? Check "Host & Compress". We'll host it on our viewer and give you a clean, easy-to-scan URL QR instead.
  • Geo Auto-Fill:Don't look up coordinates! Just type "London" or "Tokyo" in the Geo template search bar and watch the Lat/Lon fields fill automatically.
  • Context Menu:Right-click any text, link, or image on the web and select "Create QR" to instantly launch the Side Panel with that data.
