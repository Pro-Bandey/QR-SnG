🎉 Welcome to QR Code Tools

Your ultimate all-in-one suite for generating, scanning, and customizing QR codes.

Start Using Extension

🚀 Quick Overview

Thank you for installing! Our extension lives right in your browser's side panel, meaning you can use it alongside any webpage without switching tabs. Just click the extension icon in your toolbar to slide it open.

🎨 Generate & Customize

Create QR codes from URLs, Text, Wi-Fi info, vCards, Emails, and Geo-Locations. Customize the colors, gradients, dots, and eye shapes natively.

📸 Smart Scanner

Drop an image file into the extension or right-click any image on the web to instantly extract its QR code content. It auto-formats vCards and Wi-Fi networks beautifully.

🖱️ Context Menus

Highlight any text, right-click a link, or right-click an image anywhere on the web, and select our extension from the menu to instantly send that data to the side panel.

🕰️ Powerful History

Never lose a QR code again. Every scan and generation is saved to your local history. Favorite them, restore them, or let the 30-day auto-bin manage the clutter.

💡 Pro Tips & Tricks

  • Drag & Drop Magic:You can highlight text on a webpage, drag it, and drop it directly onto the extension window to generate a QR instantly!
  • Massive Text Fix:Standard QR codes fail if you type too much text. If you have a huge paragraph, check the "Host & Compress Long Text"option. We will host your text on a secure dark-mode viewer and convert the URL into a super-clean, easy-to-scan QR code.
  • Smart Auto-Fill:Using the Geo-Location template? You don't need to look up GPS coordinates. Just type a city like "Paris" in the search bar and press enter.
  • Native Exports:Need to print your QR code on a giant banner? Download it as an SVGformat for infinite vector scaling without pixelation.
